BASIC TIPS

  • Avoid unwanted pressure from the table by not sitting in the leftmost seat.
  • Always assume the dealer's face-down card is a 10 and use that to see where you stand.
  • Your only goal is to beat the dealer's hand.

COUNTING CARDS

A way you can increase your odds of winning is through counting cards. Keeping tabs on insight to how to play your hand. Counting is done by assigning cards a value.

  • -|+ 1 for 10, J, Q, K, A
  • 0 for 7, 8, 9
  • +|- 1 for 2, 3, 4, 5, 6

Whether you assign a positive value or a negative value to the 10-value cards does not matter as long as the low natural value cards are the opposite. Just be consistent. The true deck of the value (TV) will then be the "Running Count (RC)" which is the current count of all played hands divided by the number of decks (D) in play. To reiterate, TV = RC / D. In most cases, casino tables will play with more than one deck, the general amount is six decks in play. The running count should also always be reset to 0 upon shuffle.
